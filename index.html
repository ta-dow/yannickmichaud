Yannick Michaud

Yannick Michaud has a passion to create images that evoke emotion and thought. He began his career in the early 1990’s when, studying History and Literature at McGill University, in Montreal, Canada, he woke up one early spring morning and decided that he enjoyed creating images more than studying in a stuffy room.

After finishing a degree in Commercial Photography he decided to follow his Danish wife back to Denmark where he immediately began working for some of Scandinavia’s most prestigious commercial studios and had an impressive client list that entailed Lancôme, L’Oreal and Givenchy. Yannick also worked on a multitude of projects for magazines, brochures, large scale commercial work and also for the Danish Royal House.

After many years away from home, Yannick decided to return to Canada and embark on a new phase in his photographic journey, this time working for a major camera manufacturer where he held the position as Technical Sales Executive and Senior Manager of the Technical Team. Since then, he has dedicated his life to capture images from all over North America, Europe and even Japan.
